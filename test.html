Nutrition Can Help You

Proper diet helps controlling lifestyle diseases. A therapeutic nutrition reduce symptoms of diseases like diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, digestive diseases, obesity, and other disorders. Therapeutic nutrition also helps to prevent the complications of some chronic diseases.



Diseases Section

  • Hypertension

    Hypertension refers to high blood pressure. To maintain your blood pressure levels, you should maintain your food intake and reduce the amount of salt you take, and try to maintain a healthy weight. Each disease has a certin diet rules and restrictions.

    ×
  • Kidney Disease

    Dietary guidelines for kidney patients vary according to the case or the stage. You should control your intake of potassium, calcium, phosphorus, sodium, protein, fluid and calorie.

    ×
  • Diabetes

    To help you in following your diet, we provide you with a list of healthy restaurants that have your order with consideration of your diet limits.

    ×
  • IBS

    IBS refers to Irritable Bowel Syndrome. Patients with IBS should do some changes in their diet to treat the symptoms. Foods that cause pain are different from one patient to another. You should know what are the triggers that affect your colon and avoid them.

    ×
  • Celiac Disease

    Celiac disease is a digestive disorder in the small intestine. This disease is triggered by eating foods that contain Gluten.

    ×
  • Obesity

    Obesity is a medical disorder that involve an excessive amount of body fat, and it increases the risk of other health problems. you should reduce the amount of calorie you take per day to help your body to lose the excessive fat.

    ×
  • Epilepsy

    Epilepsy is a neurological disease that causes seizures. Studies suggest that following a ketogenic diet would help to reduce the seizures.
